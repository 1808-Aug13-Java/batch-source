Street Fighter V

Street Fighter will always be an iconic game. It has a really long legacy of being a competitive game. Street Fighter V is the most recent iteration of the game, specifically Street Fighter V Arcade edition. This game is the main event game for EVO. EVO is one of the most prestigious fighting game tournaments to date. Any of the champions who win EVO have a legacy now and people will remember them.
