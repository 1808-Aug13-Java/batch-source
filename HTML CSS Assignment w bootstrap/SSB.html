Super Smash Bros

Super Smash Bros is a old game that a lot of people still play. What makes this game so enjoyable for everyone is that the characters are all iconic and everyone knows who they are. Most people are familiar with Mario and the characters in his game. This game series has multiple games that are still played at a competitive level. The games that are currently in the series are Smash 64, Melee, Brawl, and Smash 4 Wii U. There will be a brand new game in the series at the end of this year. Many people are looking forward to it.
