StarCraft

StarCraft, a game developed by Blizzard Entertainment, is a real time strategy game that primarily is popular in South Korea. There are two main Starcraft games that are played competitively. There is StarCraft 2 and StarCraft Brood War. Brood War is widely considered one of the most balanced ESPORTS and requires a lot of skill to play. It also shows its strength as an ESPORT as it is still being played 20 years later. It had also recieved an HD remaster that improved its graphics but still kept all the game mechanics the same.
