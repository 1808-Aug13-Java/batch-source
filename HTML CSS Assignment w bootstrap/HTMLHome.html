ESPORTS

What is ESPORTS?

Esports stands for Electronic sports and generally spans video games. There are a lot of different games that are played competitively. It is an amazing thing to see that people can play and even become professionals at video games. Some players can even make a living off of doing this. Just as an example, the game DOTA 2 just finished its major tournament, The International, and the winning team recieved $11 million dollars.
