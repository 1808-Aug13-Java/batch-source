The Galactic Empire was established as a successor to the Galactic Republic in an effort to provide security across the Galaxy.

A political structure designed to limit the impacts or greed and corrpution in the Galactic Senate, Emperor Palpatine is able to exert more control on those who used to be able to benefit themselves at the expense of the Galaxy

After the Clone Wars, the need for a standing Army was made apparent, due to the Seperatists almost being abl to conqure the Galaxy. The Empire is working to ensure that no such movement could rise again.

In short, the Empire seeks to protect the Galaxy and its inhabitants from those who would seek to harm or destory it. The Empire leads with a gentle, but firm, lead.
