Simmy Store


For over a decade, Simmy Store has changed the landscape of sneaker retail. Carrying every brand name on the market, Simmy Store has evolved from a one-stop sneaker destination to a cultural hub for sneaker enthusiasts and novices alike. With one brick-and-mortar locations housed in Columbia, SC. Simmy Store remains the premier source for everything sneakers. From Air Jordan to Nike to Adidas and more, we have it all.
