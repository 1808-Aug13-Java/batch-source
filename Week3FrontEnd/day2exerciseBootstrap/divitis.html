What is Divitis?

Divitis is when we use too many div elements with no semantic meaning instead of the semantic tags that are available to us. It won't break your page. It won't harm your design. Instead you'll just lose semantic meaning of your document. No biggie, just not ideal. Also, when you use semantic tags, it make you look a little smarter. I'm always trying to look a little smarter than I actually am.
