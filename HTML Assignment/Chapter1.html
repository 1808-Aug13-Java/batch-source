Chapter 1: The Seven

Once there was a land that lived in fear of seven goblins.

They were called the Rainbow Goblins and each had his own color which was also his name!

Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Violet. Yellow being the craftiest was their chief.

The goblins lived on color, they prowled the valleys and climbed the highest mountains looking for rainbows!

And when they found one, they caught it in their lassos, sucked the colors out of it and filled their bellies with the bright liquid
