Chapter 2: The Valley

Only one place in the land had never known goblin fear: the hidden valley called the Valley of the Rainbow.

Where the great arches of color were born. There the animals still lived in paradise.

But the Rainbow Goblins had heard tales of this valley and their mouths watered whenever they thought of the feast that awaited them there.

And so they gathered up their lassos and pails and set off.
