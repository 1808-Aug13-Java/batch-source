Reimbursement Request

Company policy reimburses travel for work at a flat rate of $100 for travel under 50 miles, and a flat rate of 250 for travel greater than 50 miles. If you travel beyond 150 miles for work your manager will have you fill out a different form that will have been pre-approved for an increased budget. Training reimbursement is provided at a flat rate for any course work or conventions attended. If you feel the need to spend more than $60 dollars on approved supplies such us records, lunch for entertaining clients, general office supplies, etc... you will need to submit a more detailed form in paper to your manager.
